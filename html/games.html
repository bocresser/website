Coding

How to play the game: use the arrow keys to move the Earth left and right (touch the left and right side of the screen if on a mobile device). Hit the spacebar to get the smaller Earth to move and the game to begin. When the smaller Earth hits a polititian, they are knocked from the cabinet. If the smaller Earth goes below the screen, or the larger Earth is hit by a falling politician, you lose a life. If you take down all the politicians before losing three lives yourself, you win the game! Click on the image to play.
